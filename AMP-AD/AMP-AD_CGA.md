# Summary of AMP-AD analysis regarding Chromogranin A (CgA)

## AMP-AD
+ Regarding the dataset information, please go to this [page](https://github.com/ningxinkang/Chen_lab_analysis/blob/main/AMP-AD/AMP-AD_limma_diagnosis.md#1-dataset-info)!!
+ T-test and three-way ANOVA show consistent significant down-regulation (AD vs. control) in brain region BM36 (Parahippocampal gyrus)
+ No significant difference found when splitting samples using braak stage, but we can see a slight decrease in expression level of CHGA in samples with **braak level 4-6 vs. braak level 0-3** in Superior temporal gyrus (BM22) and Parahippocampal gyrus (BM36). This two region also show significant down-regulated in t-test and 3-way ANOVA.
+ DE analysis ran by Limma pipeline agree with t-test and 3-way anova result! We saw significant down-regulation ([AD vs. control](https://github.com/ningxinkang/Chen_lab_analysis/blob/main/AMP-AD/AMP-AD_CGA.md#dignosis)) of CHGA in BM22 and BM36. And this down-regulation pattern is also observed in female cohort BM36 when [considering sex as an effect] (https://github.com/ningxinkang/Chen_lab_analysis/blob/main/AMP-AD/AMP-AD_CGA.md#dignosis).
+ However, we don't see any significant difference when looking at APOE4 carrier or APOE dose. In other words, when comparing APOE 4 carrier (sample with at least 1 copy of APOE 4 allele) vs. no carrier (samples with no copy of APOE 4 allele), and when comparing 2-copies vs. 1-copy APOE 4/ 1 vs. 0/ 2 vs. 0, **the expression level of CHGA stays NO DIFFERENCES**. (One thing worth noticing is that, the uneven distribution of number of samples in each category - please check carrier data info [here](https://github.com/ningxinkang/Chen_lab_analysis/blob/main/AMP-AD/AMP-AD_CGA.md#dignosis) and dose info [here] (https://github.com/ningxinkang/Chen_lab_analysis/blob/main/AMP-AD/AMP-AD_limma_APOE_dose.md#1-dataset-info)- I think the result does not have strong statistical significance).
